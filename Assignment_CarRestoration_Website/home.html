About Jack Jetson

Hi! I'm Jack Jetson, and restoring classic Mini Coopers isn't just a hobby - it's my passion. There's nothing quite like bringing these iconic cars back to life, one bolt appreciate the craftsmanship, get inspired, or maybe start your own restoration journey, take a look around. I'd love to share the stories behind these timeless classics and my process in making them shine again.

I started my journey in the world of classic cars many years ago, and ever since, my love for these vintage gems has only grown. After restoring my first Mini, I realized how much joy it brought to both myself and the owners of these restored beauties. The pride in bringing a car back to its original glory is truly unmatched.

My workshop is fully equipped with the latest tools and technologies, allowing me to provide top-quality restoration services. Whether it's repairing rust, restoring the interior, or rebuilding engines, I focus on every small detail to ensure the car is restored to perfection. I also love working with customers to help them restore their cars with the utmost care and attention to detail, making sure that each project is unique.

Over the years, I've had the pleasure of working on many classic Mini Coopers, but I don't just stop there. I've worked on several other classic cars, each with its own challenges and stories. With each project, I continue to refine my skills and learn something new, which makes each car a new and exciting challenge.

I invite you to take a look at my gallery and some of the restoration projects I've worked on. If you share the same passion for these iconic cars, I'd love to connect and discuss how we can work together on your restoration journey.

About Our Restoration Team

At Jetson's Classic Restorations, we believe in more than just fixing cars - we believe in reviving pieces of automotive history. Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional restoration services for classic Mini Coopers, vintage cars, and automotive treasures. We understand that every car has a story, and we're here to bring that story back to life with precision, care, and passion.

The restoration journey isn't just about repairing parts; it's about rediscovering the heart of each vehicle. Our founder, Jack Jetson, started this journey with a passion for classic cars and a vision to bring lost gems back to life. Over the years, we have grown into a full team of expert technicians, each with a specialized skill set, from bodywork to engine restoration, upholstery to painting, and everything in between. We take immense pride in every car we restore.

Our team is committed to quality and attention to detail. From the first consultation to the final polish, we work closely with our clients to understand their vision and ensure that every project is carried out with the highest standards. We believe in transparency, so we keep our clients updated throughout the process, making sure you're the privilege of working on some truly remarkable cars. Each project is a new challenge, and it's what drives us to continually push the boundaries of what we can achieve. We are constantly refining our techniques and staying up-to-date with the latest automotive technologies, ensuring that our restorations not only look great but run like new.

Whether you're restoring a classic Mini Cooper or a rare vintage car, we understand how important it is to maintain the original charm while ensuring modern durability. Our goal is to provide the best restoration service possible, offering our expertise and love for classic cars to help you bring your project to life.
